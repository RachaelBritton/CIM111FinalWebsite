On this page I pay tribute to the main inspirations for my work. As a student artist, I believe it is important to share the work of other artists, in order to help people like me learn about different art styles and techniques.

Aaron Blaise

Aaron Blaise is an illustrator and animator who worked with Walt Disney Feature Animation for 21 years. He has worked on feature films including "Mulan", "Aladdin", "The Lion King", and was the co-director of "Brother Bear". He offers free and paid tutorials and lessons on his website, www.creatureartteacher.com.

Tom Bancroft

Tom Bancroft is an illustrator and animator who has illustrated over 50 children's books and has over 25 years of experience in the animation industry. He has worked on feature films including "Beauty and the Beast", "The Lion King", "Mulan", and has also worked with Big Idea Productions, the creators of the popular "VeggieTales" animated series. He sells adult coloring books and other teaching guides on his website, http://tombancroftstudio.com

Fabiola Garza

Fabiola Garza is an illustrator currently working as a Character Artist at Disney Design Group in Orlando, Florida. She has studied at Rhode Island School of Design and Ringling College of Art and Design. She recently published her first children's book, "The Story of Saint John Paul II: A Boy Who Became Pope." You can learn more about Fabiola, her books, her art, and events on her blog, http://fabisart.blogspot.com
